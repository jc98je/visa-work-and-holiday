📐 Gramática Básica

Presente Simple +

Uso: Hábitos, rutinas, hechos generales.

Estructura: Sujeto + verbo (s/es en 3ra persona)

✅ I work from home. ✅ She plays tennis. ✅ The train leaves at 8am.

Adverbios de frecuencia: always, usually, often, sometimes, rarely, never

Posición: Antes del verbo principal, después de "be"

✅ I always wake up early. ✅ She is never late.

✏️ Ejercicio — Completa con presente simple:

1. She in a bank.

2. I breakfast at 7am.

3. He tennis every weekend.

4. The bus at 9am.

5. She like coffee. (negativo)

Pasado Simple +

Uso: Acciones terminadas en el pasado con momento definido.

Estructura: Sujeto + verbo pasado (-ed o irregular)

✅ I visited Cusco last year. ✅ She went to the bank yesterday. ✅ We watched a movie.

Verbos irregulares comunes:

PresentePasadoPresentePasado
gowenteatate
seesawbuybought
comecametaketook
havehadmakemade

✏️ Ejercicio — Pasado simple:

1. I Cusco last year.

2. She to the market yesterday.

3. We pizza last night.

4. He a new car last week.

Futuro (Going to / Will) +

Going to: Planes e intenciones futuras

I am going to study English this weekend. (plan)

Will: Decisiones espontáneas, predicciones

I will help you with that. (decisión espontánea)

¿Cuándo usar cada uno?

Plan: "I am going to travel to Australia next year."
Decisión: "The phone is ringing — I will answer it."

✏️ Ejercicio — Going to o Will:

1. I visit my family next week. (plan)

2. It's cold — I close the window. (decisión)

3. She start university in September. (plan)

Artículos y Singular/Plural +

A/An: indefinite (a book, an apple)

The: definite (the book on the table)

Plural: book→books, child→children, man→men, woman→women

I need a pen. The pen is on the table. I have two children.

✏️ Ejercicio — Completa con a/an/the:

1. I need pen.

2. sun is very hot today.

3. She is engineer.

🎧 Listening Básico

Sección 1: Conversaciones cotidianas — reservar hotel, preguntar por precios, hacer una cita.

  • Escucha palabras clave: números, nombres, fechas.
  • Respeta el límite de palabras: "NO MORE THAN TWO WORDS".
  • Si no escuchas algo, sigue adelante — no te quedes pensando.

📖 Reading Básico

Texto: "Maria works in a bank in Lima. She starts work at 9am and finishes at 5pm. She likes her job because she enjoys helping people. On weekends, she usually visits her family."

🗣️ Speaking — Part 1

Estrategia

Respuestas de 2-4 oraciones. Añade siempre una razón.

Pregunta: "What do you usually do in your free time?"
Respuesta modelo: "In my free time, I mostly work on personal projects and take photos. I find it really relaxing because it takes my mind off work."

Otras preguntas:

  • "Where are you from?" — "I'm from Lima, Peru. It's a big city with lots of traffic!"
  • "Do you like your job?" — "Yes, I do. I enjoy helping people every day."
  • "What did you do last weekend?" — "I visited my family and we had lunch together."

📐 Gramática Intermedia

Present Perfect vs Past Simple +
Present PerfectPast Simple
I have visited Australia. (experiencia)I visited Australia in 2023. (momento)
She has lived here for 3 years. (duración)She lived in Lima for 2 years. (terminó)
Regla: Present Perfect = sin momento definido. Past Simple = con momento definido (yesterday, last week, in 2020).

✏️ Ejercicio — Present Perfect o Past Simple:

1. I Australia. (experiencia)

2. I Australia in 2023. (momento)

3. She here for 3 years. (duración)

4. We to the beach last Sunday.

Condicionales 1 y 2 +

1er condicional (posible): If + presente, will + infinitivo

If it rains tomorrow, I will stay home.

2do condicional (hipotético): If + pasado, would + infinitivo

If I had more money, I would travel to Australia.

✏️ Ejercicio:

1. If it , I will stay home.

2. If I more time, I would travel.

3. If she harder, she would pass.

Voz Pasiva +

Estructura: Sujeto + be + pasado participio

Active: They built the bridge in 1998.
Passive: The bridge was built in 1998.

✏️ Ejercicio — Activa o Pasiva:

1. The bridge in 1998. (pasiva)

2. English in many countries. (pasiva)

3. They a new school. (activa)

Reported Speech +

"I am tired," she said. → She said she was tired.

Cambios: am→was, will→would, can→could, today→that day, tomorrow→the next day

✏️ Ejercicio:

1. "I happy." → She said she happy.

2. "I help you." → He said he help me.

📐 Gramática Avanzada

3er Condicional y Mixed +

3er condicional: If + pasado perfecto, would have + participio

If I had studied harder, I would have passed the exam.

Mixed conditional: Mezcla pasado y presente

If I had studied medicine (pasado), I would be a doctor now (presente).

✏️ Ejercicio:

1. If I harder, I would have passed.

2. If she earlier, she would have caught the bus.

3. If I that job, I would be richer now.

Cláusulas Relativas +
RelativoUsoEjemplo
whichCosasThe book, which I borrowed, is good.
who/whomPersonasThe scientist, who won the award.
whosePosesiónThe student whose essay won.
whereLugaresThe university where I studied.

✏️ Ejercicio:

1. The book, I borrowed, is interesting. (cosa)

2. The student essay won is here. (posesión)

3. The university I studied is in Sydney. (lugar)

Participle Clauses +
Having finished the report, she submitted it.
Influenced by globalisation, cultures are changing.
Located in the south, the city enjoys mild weather.

Estas estructuras hacen el texto más fluido y académico — útiles en Writing Task 2.

Cleft Sentences +
It was technology that changed everything.
What concerns me is the impact on children.
The reason why I left was the low salary.

Enfatizan un elemento — muy útiles en Writing y Speaking para sonar más natural.

✍️ Writing — Task 2

Estructura
  • Introducción: Parafrasear + thesis statement.
  • Párrafo 1-2: Ideas + ejemplos específicos.
  • Conclusión: Resumen sin repetir literalmente.
Conectores
  • furthermore, however, consequently, for instance

🗣️ Speaking — Part 3

Preguntas analíticas. Usa: opinión → razón → ejemplo → matización.

Pregunta: "Has technology made people more isolated?"
Respuesta: "To some extent, yes. While it has made communication more convenient, it could be argued that it has reduced face-to-face interaction."

🎯 Práctica Final — Nivel 3

1. 3er Condicional:

"If I harder, I would have passed."

2. Cláusula relativa:

"The book, I borrowed, is good."

3. Escribe una thesis: "Universities should focus on practical skills."
