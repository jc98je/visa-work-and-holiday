📐 Gramática Básica

Presente Simple +

Uso: Hábitos, rutinas, hechos generales.

✅ I work from home. ✅ She plays tennis. ✅ The train leaves at 8am.

Adverbios: always, usually, often, sometimes, rarely, never

Pasado Simple +

Uso: Acciones terminadas con momento definido.

✅ I visited Cusco last year. ✅ She went to the bank yesterday.

Señales: yesterday, last week, ago, in 2020

Futuro con "Going to" y "Will" +

Going to: Planes → I am going to study English.

Will: Decisiones espontáneas → I will help you.

Artículos y Singular/Plural +

A/An: a book, an apple | The: the book on the table

I need a pen. The pen is on the table.

🎧 Listening Básico

Sección 1: Conversaciones cotidianas — reservar hotel, preguntar por precios.

Estrategia
  • Escucha palabras clave: números, nombres, fechas.
  • Respeta el límite de palabras: "NO MORE THAN TWO WORDS".

Completa con la palabra correcta:

"I'd like to book a room for nights, please."

"Could I have your and phone number?"

📖 Reading Básico

Texto: "Maria works in a bank in Lima. She starts work at 9am and finishes at 5pm. She likes her job because she enjoys helping people."

🗣️ Speaking Básico — Part 1

Preguntas personales: trabajo, estudios, hobbies, rutina.

Estrategia
  • Respuestas de 2-4 oraciones — añade siempre una razón.
  • Usa presente simple para hábitos: "I usually work from home."
Pregunta: "What do you usually do in your free time?"
Respuesta: "In my free time, I mostly work on projects. I find it relaxing."

📐 Gramática Intermedia

Present Perfect vs Past Simple +
Present PerfectPast Simple
I have visited Australia.I visited Australia in 2023.
She has lived here for 3 years.She lived in Lima for 2 years.
Condicionales 1 y 2 +

1er: If + presente, will + infinitivo → If it rains, I will stay home.

2do: If + pasado, would + infinitivo → If I had money, I would travel.

Voz Pasiva +
Active: They built the bridge in 1998.
Passive: The bridge was built in 1998.
Reported Speech +

Direct: "I am tired," she said. → Reported: She said she was tired.

Cambios: am→was, will→would, can→could, today→that day

🎧 Listening Intermedio

Sección 2-3: Monólogos y discusiones — guías turísticos, clases, entrevistas.

Estrategia
  • Identifica la actitud del hablante: segura, dudosa, entusiasta.
  • Cuidado con los distractores — mencionan opciones parecidos antes de la correcta.

📖 Reading Intermedio

Texto: "Urban green spaces have been shown to reduce stress levels among city residents, although researchers note that the effect varies depending on the size and accessibility of the park."

✍️ Writing — Task 1

Objetivo

Describe gráficos, tablas o procesos objetivamente — sin opinión personal.

Estructura: Introducción → Overview → Detalles

Lenguaje útil
  • Tendencias: rise, increase, grow / fall, decline, drop
  • Velocidad: sharply, steadily, gradually, slightly
  • Comparación: compared to, whereas, in contrast
Overview: "Overall, Country A experienced far more rapid growth than Country B."

🗣️ Speaking — Part 2 (Cue Card)

1 min preparación + 1-2 min hablando sobre un tema.

Cómo preparar
  • Anota 3-4 palabras clave por cada punto de la tarjeta.
  • No escribas oraciones completas — solo ideas guía.
Ejemplo: "Describe a piece of technology you find useful."

📐 Gramática Avanzada

3er Condicional y Mixed +

3er: If + pasado perfecto, would have + participio → If I had studied, I would have passed.

Mixed: If I had studied medicine (pasado), I would be a doctor now (presente).

Cláusulas Relativas Avanzadas +
RelativoUsoEjemplo

✍️ Writing — Task 2 (Ensayo)

Estructura
  • Introducción: Parafrasear + thesis statement.
  • Párrafo 1: Idea principal + explicación + ejemplo.
  • Párrafo 2: Segunda idea + explicación + ejemplo.
  • Conclusión: Resumen sin repetir literalmente.
Conectores
  • Añadir: furthermore, moreover, in addition
  • Contrastar: however, nevertheless, conversely
  • Consecuencia: consequently, therefore, thus
Thesis: "I believe the advantages of remote work outweigh its drawbacks."

🗣️ Speaking — Part 3 (Discusión)

Preguntas analíticas. Usa: opinión → razón → ejemplo → matización.

Vocabulario clave
  • Opinión: from my perspective, arguably, it's widely believed that
  • Matización: to some extent, not necessarily, it depends on
Pregunta: "Do you think technology has made people more isolated?"
Respuesta: "To some extent, yes. While technology has made communication more convenient, it could be argued that it has reduced face-to-face interaction."
