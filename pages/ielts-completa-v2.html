📐 Gramática Básica

Presente Simple +

Uso: Hábitos, rutinas, hechos generales.

✅ I work from home. ✅ She plays tennis.

Adverbios: always, usually, often, sometimes, rarely, never

Pasado Simple +

Uso: Acciones terminadas con momento definido.

✅ I visited Cusco last year.

Señales: yesterday, last week, ago, in 2020

Futuro +

Going to: Planes → I am going to study English.

Will: Decisiones → I will help you.

🎧 Listening Básico

Sección 1: Conversaciones cotidianas — reservar hotel, preguntar por precios.

Estrategia
  • Escucha palabras clave: números, nombres, fechas.
  • Respeta el límite de palabras: "NO MORE THAN TWO WORDS".

Completa:

"I'd like to book a room for nights."

"Could I have your and phone number?"

📖 Reading Básico

Texto: "Maria works in a bank in Lima. She starts at 9am and finishes at 5pm. She enjoys helping people."

🗣️ Speaking — Part 1

Pregunta: "What do you usually do in your free time?"
Respuesta: "In my free time, I mostly work on projects. I find it relaxing."

📐 Gramática Intermedia

Present Perfect vs Past Simple +
Present PerfectPast Simple
I have visited Australia.I visited Australia in 2023.
She has lived here for 3 years.She lived in Lima for 2 years.
Condicionales 1 y 2 +

1er: If + presente, will → If it rains, I will stay home.

2do: If + pasado, would → If I had money, I would travel.

Voz Pasiva +
Active: They built the bridge. → Passive: The bridge was built.
Reported Speech +

"I am tired" → She said she was tired.

Cambios: am→was, will→would, can→could

🎧 Listening Intermedio

Sección 2-3: Monólogos y discusiones.

Estrategia
  • Identifica la actitud del hablante.
  • Cuidado con los distractores.

📖 Reading Intermedio

✍️ Writing — Task 1

Objetivo

Describe gráficos objetivamente — sin opinión.

Lenguaje útil
  • Tendencias: rise, increase, fall, decline
  • Velocidad: sharply, steadily, gradually

🗣️ Speaking — Part 2

1 min preparación + 1-2 min hablando.

Ejemplo: "Describe a piece of technology you find useful."

📐 Gramática Avanzada

3er Condicional y Mixed +

3er: If + pasado perfecto, would have + participio → If I had studied, I would have passed.

Mixed: If I had studied medicine, I would be a doctor now.

Cláusulas Relativas +
RelativoUsoEjemplo
whichCosasThe book, which I borrowed, is good.
whoPersonasThe scientist, who won the award.
whosePosesiónThe student whose essay won.
whereLugaresThe university where I studied.
Participle Clauses +
Having finished the report, she submitted it.
Influenced by globalisation, cultures are changing.
Cleft Sentences +
It was technology that changed everything.
What concerns me is the impact on children.

✍️ Writing — Task 2

Estructura
  • Introducción: Parafrasear + thesis.
  • Párrafo 1-2: Ideas + ejemplos.
  • Conclusión: Resumen.
Conectores
  • furthermore, however, consequently, for instance

🗣️ Speaking — Part 3

Preguntas analíticas. Usa: opinión → razón → ejemplo → matización.

Pregunta: "Has technology made people more isolated?"
Respuesta: "To some extent, yes. While it has made communication more convenient, it could be argued that it has reduced face-to-face interaction."

🎯 Práctica Final — Nivel 3

1. Completa el 3er condicional:

"If I harder, I would have passed."

2. Cláusula relativa correcta:

The book, ___ I borrowed
which (cosas)
The student ___ essay won
whose (posesión)

3. Escribe una thesis statement para: "Some people believe universities should focus on practical skills."
